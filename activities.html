Outdoor Games

Football

Football, being one of the most approachable games in the Institute, has had the most numbers of tournaments, including Inter-NIT and Intra NITS tournaments.
INTER NIT SPORTS MEET AT NIT WARANGAL
Team NIT Silchar for the first time participated in the inter nit football meet at NIT Warangal and reached the quarter finals.
The Intra NIT competitions include-
  • FRESHERS FOOTBALL NIGHT TOURNAMENT
  • NITS PREMIER LEAGUE
  • 6 ON 6 FUTSAL TOURNAMENT , INCANDESCENCE
  • FIFA 14 GAMING EVENT , TECNOESIS
  • INTER HOSTEL FOOTBALL NIGHT TOURNAMENT
NIT Silchar also takes part in IIT GUWAHATI SPORTS MEET during ALCHERINGA

Basketball

Slowly but steadily the game of Basketball is making a name in Indian households for its fast paced play style and NIT Silchar recognizes that enthusiasm in the players and encourages them to take the game to new heights. The Tournaments and major practice sessions at NIT Silchar are as follows-
  • Inter Hostel Night Tournament
  • Inter NIT practice sessions and Drills

Cricket

Being an institute in India and lacking in Cricket facility is as absurd as putting together the broken pieces of vase. In the same way, the cricket facilities of NIT Silchar are like the flowers in the vase of Sports Club. The NIT Silchar has an extravaganza of Cricketing Tournaments to meet the ever rising expectations of the Cricket enthusiasts and players alike. NIT Silchar takes part or organises the following events-
  • Inter Hostel Night Tournament
  • Inter NIT Tournaments (For Last 3 years in places like Trichy, Allahabad etc.)
  • Ekta Cup in Hostel 7(Mini Night Tournament)

Lawn Tennis

The Lawn Tennis is a game the Institute is working upon the most of late and very soon the Institute would be a hub of all Lawn Tennis lovers of the Institute. The Competitions organized at NIT Silchar are-
  • Inter Hostel Tournament
  • Inter NIT Trials

Kabaddi and Kho-Kho

NIT Silchar boasts of a very rich sporting culture in Kabaddi and Kho-Kho, having a well maintained clay court of internationally approved dimensions, regular inter hostel and inter competitions are being organized in the premises. A college Kabaddi team is particularly trained and prepared for representing the college in various inter-college competitions like Inter-NIT Sports Meet, where the team members are selected from the best Kabaddi players of the college. Regular Kabaddi sessions are organized by the Hockey & Kabaddi Secretary of the Gymkhana Students’ Union Body under the guidance of Sports Secretary and Sports Officer
The college team is a well-crafted and rigorously trained team on an amateur level, consisting of good raiders, defenders and all-rounders. The freshmen students are taught about the basics of the games right from the beginning with practice matches being held on regular intervals to check the growth the students in the sports.

Athletics and Gym

One of the most vibrant clubs of NIT Silchar is its Athletics and Gymnastics Club, conducting several thrilling events on workout and field & track related sports. This club is responsible for conducting fitness events like body building competition, powerlifting, and arm wrestling along with athletics events like sprinting, marathon run, javelin throw, shot put throw, discus throw and cycling; throughout the year. Recently, this club has started Yoga training in the campus which attracted a lot of participation.
